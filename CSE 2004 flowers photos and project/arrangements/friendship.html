Friendship

Assemble this bouquet for a best friend, as a celebration of your friendship, to brighten their day, or to let them know they are on your mind.

Fastened With
Teal chiffon ribbon
Combine
  • Zinnia for everlasting friendship
  • Apple blossom for preference
  • Pansy for a friend you think of often
  • Eucalyptus for protection and to strengthen the bond of friendship, so that it may last throughout the years
Use For
A celebration of friendship, a brightening gesture, or a thoughtful reminder that someone is on your mind.
