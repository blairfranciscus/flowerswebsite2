Begonia

Begonia

Victoria's Definition: Caution

Meaning
  • To repay a favor
  • A warning
Origin
To repay a favor, Charles Plumier, a seventeenth-century French botanist, named the begonia flower after Michel Begon, a French politician and plant collector. The flower's name, which contains the phrase "be gone," may explain its use as a symbol of warning.
Pair With
  • Sweet Pea as a gift for the host of a party
  • Oleander to emphasize caution in a new prospect
