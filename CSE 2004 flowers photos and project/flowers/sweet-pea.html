Sweet Pea

Lathyrus odoratus

Victoria's Definition: Delicate pleasures

Meaning
Thank you for a lovely time
Origin
Victorians gave sweet peas to thank their hosts for an enjoyable time. The flower's light and sweet smell was believed to brighten the home and serve as a symbol of hospitality.
Pair With
  • Hyssop and Orchid to thank a friend for inviting you to their home
  • Zinnia as a token of appreciation
